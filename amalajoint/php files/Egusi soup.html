Melon (Egusi) Soup



The major ingredients are

Ground melon
Smoked turkey
Smoked Mackerel
Salt
Beef stock cubes
Nigerian pepper mix
Dried Prawns
Palm Oil
Spinach


Method of preparation

Step 1: Add ground melon to a bowl, add about a cup of water and mix to form a paste then set aside. Wash and chop the spinach, drain in a colander and set aside
Step 2: Place a big pan on medium heat, add palm oil and heat for about 3 minutes (Do not bleach oil) add the reserved chopped onions and Sautee till translucent
step 3: Add pepper mix, locust beans and stir to combine. Bring to boil for 5 minutes.
Step 4: Add the egusi paste in bits to pepper, reduce the heat, do not stir and cover the pot with a lid. Cook for another 10 minutes.
step 5: Remove the lid, and gently stir the soup. The egusi would be lumpy at this point, use the back of a ladle to break the lumps it into desired size/texture
Step 6: Add smoked turkey, ground crayfish and crayfish, beef stock (if using) and stir to combine. Add bouillon cubes, taste and adjust accordingly. step 7: Add shredded smoked mackerel and gently stir into the soup. Add chopped spinach, stir to combine and cook for another 3 to 5 minutes. Take it off the heat, allow to cool for about 10 minutes before serving
